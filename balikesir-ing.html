  • Spas

    • There are many underground resources in Balıkesir. The most important of these are thermal spas and natural water resources. Many thermal hotels, baths and hot springs are said to heal in the city and attract many visitors even from outside the city.

  • Antandros Antic City

    • Although it has lost its historical texture, Antandros is one of the ancient cities that has managed to preserve many architectural monuments and historical parts. This ancient city, which has become one of the symbols of Balıkesir, has been one of the Episcopal centers in Christianity for many years throughout history.

  • Cunda Island

    • This island, which you will arrive by boat in a very short time, contains architecture and culture that can give you a complete medieval atmosphere. When you arrive on this island, we recommend that you spend at least a few nights and chat with the island people in the coffee shop.
