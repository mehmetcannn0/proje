    Best Water Sports in Antalya

  • 1-Rafting In Köprülü Canyon

    • Antalya can be called the center of the sport of rafting in Turkey. Köprüçay makes this sport more enjoyable, especially in the region known as Köprülü Canyon.

  • 2-Scuba Diving-Diving in Altınbeşik Cave

    • Antalya is not only among the favorites of diving enthusiasts thanks to Suluin, the largest cave in Europe. The city, where our country's most popular holiday routes are located, is also the 3rd largest in the world. the great underwater cave is home to Altınbeşik. The underground formation within the borders of ibradi district bears the title of the first and only Cave National Park of our country.

  • 3-Canyoning in Kapuştaş

    • Canyons located in Antalya are among the priority preferences of those who want to participate in canyoning activities in Turkey.

  • 4-Wakeboard

    • Hip-Notics located in Antalya provides its guests with the opportunity to make cable wakeboard, water ski, kneeboard, wakeskate and monoski for 11 months thanks to its seasonal advantage in 3 different artificial lakes. Covering a fairly large area of 200, 000m2, comfortable and hotel-quality accommodation is also provided on site.

  • 5-Sandboard in Kaş

    • Sandboard is a sport that is unfortunately not common in Turkey. One of the rare spots where you can sandboard in Turkey is Patara Beach in Antalya - Kas. Located near the ancient city of Patara, the length of this beach is 12 km, while the depth of the beach can reach 200-300 meters in some places. You can sandboard in the sand dunes here.

  • Aspendos Ancient City

    • Aspendos Ancient City, 2 BC. it was built by the Romans in the century and is one of the most beautifully preserved theatres in history. This theater was built in the form of an open-air theater and the construction of the theater began in approximately 138 years. It is among the rare structures that can be preserved to this day. The architect of the theater is Zenon, the son of Theodoros of Aspendos. Today, this theater hosts many various concerts.

  • Ancient City Of Perge

    • Perge ancient city is located in Aksu district, 17 kilometers east of Antalya city center. The city, which is believed to have existed in the Hittite period and is known as "Parha", was one of the most regular cities in Anatolia during the Roman period. The sculptures unearthed during the excavations in the ancient city, which is famous for its architecture and marble sculpture, have made the Antalya Museum one of the most important sculpture museums.
