  • Anitkabir

    • At the very beginning of the places to visit when it comes to Ankara, of course, comes Anitkabir. Gazi Mustafa Kemal's eternal resting place, the mausoleum is an architectural structure befitting the glory and personality of the Great Commander.

  • Ankara Castle
  • Ethnographic Museum

    • You can visit the Ethnographic Museum to see the change and development of Turkish culture that has been going on since centuries. There are 11 halls in the museum. Apart from the extensive accumulation that it has, the main factor that makes the museum valuable for us is that the first 15 years of eternal sleep that Atatürk slept in were spent here. Due to its central location, the museum is located in Sıhhiye, one of the oldest places in Ankara. The entrance is 14 TL.

  • Kocatepe Mosque
  • Rahmi Koc Museum

    • Rahmi M., The only industrial-themed museum in Ankara Koç Museum is one of the places that deserves a visit in Ankara. In addition to industry, the museum also has a very large collection in the field of transport and communication. The main place to visit in the museum is Vehbi Koç shop. Apart from this, Ismail Uncle workshop and Ali Riza Pharmacy will also be visited from other departments.

  • Haci Bayram Veli Mosque
  • Ankara Tava

    • Ankara tava, which is delicious as well as its appearance, is one of the local dishes of the city that is loved and eaten. Although we call it a simple dish for the Ankara pan, once you taste it, you will feel the real flavor behind this simplicity. The ingredients that make up this delicious dish are noodles, bone meat, natural, peppers and tomatoes.

  • Orman Kebabı

    • Ankara's second kebab dish on our list. There is no in. We can almost say that it is a dish of flavor bomb. We can say that it is really a ball of flavor for Ankara-style forest kebab. Forest kebab, made in different varieties in many regions of our country, is made in Ankara by frying cubed meat and baking it in a casserole once. No matter what procedure is done by most people, we recommend that you try the forest kebab, which is always loved, in Ankara.

  • Yemlik Cacığı

    • Manger cacık, an indispensable type of cacık in summer days, is a type of cold drink in which manger grass and yogurt are made together. After being sweetened with garlic and liquid oil before serving, the Manger, which is ready to eat, takes on a taste that will make a person eat their fingers.
