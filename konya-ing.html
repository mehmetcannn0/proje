  • Catalhoyuk Neolithic City

    • Catalhoyuk, described as one of the oldest settlements of the Neolithic period, sheds light on human history with its original finds such as first house architecture, first landscape painting, cult of the mother goddess and works of faithst house architecture, first landscape painting, mother goddess cult and works of faith. Catalhoyuk, located in Çumra District of Konya province, was discovered in 1958. It is believed that Catalhoyuk's Neolithic settlement style and urban plan reflect the ideals of equality. As of 2012, Catalhoyuk has been included in the UNESCO World Heritage list.

  • Kilistra Antic City

    • Kilistra ancient city', located on the historical 'King Road' in Konya and drawing attention with its fairy chimneys-like structure in Cappadocia, takes its visitors on a historical journey. Kilistra, which has structures such as a cross-planned chapel, hyacinth church, large water cistern, churches, monasteries, watchtowers, shelters, ancient roads, is also an important center of faith tourism.
